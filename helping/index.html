Helping Us

The Network desires to gain resources that will continue to be used to build Christ’s church for years to come—even after we are gone and until Jesus returns. Our resources are God’s resources and there may be no better use than to have a portion of them given to Christ as He builds His church.

The Network is primarily supported by its constituent churches. Individuals may give directly to the Network or may make bequests through the Presbyterian Church in America Foundation where an endowment for the Network is established. Our working relationship with the PCA Foundation also gives us the ability to work with all kinds of trusts and other estate-planning vehicles. These provide some great ways to be faithful stewards of the wealth God has given you, to preserve it, and most of all, to give far more resources to the Lord’s work than you might imagine.

We always welcome prayer support for our ministry and for the church planters in the field.
