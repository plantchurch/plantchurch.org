Ways to Support Our Ministries

Estate and Tax Planning

The Network has established an endowment with the Presbyterian Church in America Foundation. The PCAF is an official denominational agency. Please contact us so that we can assist you with your planning.

Direct Gifts

Direct gifts may also be made to the Network or to the endowment fund at the PCAF. Checks should be made payable to the Southwest Church Planting Network with a designation if the funds are for the endowment.

Prayer Support

We covet the prayer support of individuals and churches as we work to extend the Kingdom. Please email us or drop us a letter so that we may be encouraged by your support.
