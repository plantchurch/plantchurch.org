Our Vision

It is our vision to plant 75 new churches in the Southwest by the year 2020. In addition, we are seeking to place a Reformed University Fellowship on each major college campus in the Southwest.

In addition, member churches will continue to plant new churches throughout North America and around the world outside the framework of the Network.
