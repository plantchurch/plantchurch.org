Works in Progress

Allen, Texas

Joel Miller is in the process of planting The Summit, a new PCA church in Allen, Texas. If you know of individuals moving to Allen or have friends there that would be interested in a new PCA church, you can email Joel.

Amarillo, Texas

Chris and Kandice Thomas are the church planters for Redeemer Presbyterian Church, a budding congregation in Amarillo. The congregation features a simple worship style with the preaching of God’s Word as the focus each Sunday. They are focusing on raising up faithful Christian men to lead in their young congregation. Chris can be reached at 806-220-7781. They are worshiping together on Sunday mornings at 10:30 AM and also at 6:00 PM at the Chapel of First Christian Church, located at 3001 Wolflin in Amarillo.

Dallas, Texas

St. Thomas the Doubter is led by Pastor Saji George and is currently meeting for Sunday morning worship. Their congregation currently hosts a broad mix of members and visitors.

Duncanville, Texas

Pastor Patrick Lafferty leads Christ the King in Duncanville, Texas. The congregation is a unique mix of different types of people, coming together in worship to God. They are currently meeting at 200 Carr Ln. in Duncanville at the Fairview Baptist Church at 9:30 AM on Sunday morning for worship.

Edmond, Oklahoma

Pastor Pete Hatton leads Redeemer Presbyterian Church in Edmond. They are currently focusing on raising up godly men to lead in the future life of their congregation. They meet for Sunday morning worship in Edmond at 10:30 AM at the Edmond Christian Academy. For more information about the group, please call Pete at 405-562-1350.

El Paso, Texas

Las Tierras Community Church is a mission church of the Presbytery of the Southwest. They are currently enjoying the process of expanding their ministries beyond Sunday morning offerings and are moving out into new areas of ministering to El Paso. Their congregation is a healthy mix of military families and other local El Paso natives. Manuel “Tito” Padilla and his wife, Kim, are the church planters. For more information about the church, call Tito at 915-740-9207. If you would rather, you can email Tito.

Killeen/Ft. Hood, Texas

Lou and Shirley Best are church planters in Killeen, Texas where they minister to the local community as well as the servicemen and their families from Ft. Hood. Phoenix, Arizona

New City Phoenix is a mercy church in South Phoenix. They are excited to partner with Pastor Pablo from Rose of Sharon Church where they work together to impact the South Ranch Habitat Community. New City Phoenix hosts tutoring programs and other ministries in the area. The church is meeting regularly for worship services on Sundays at 5 PM at the Rio Vista Center which is located just west of the Safeway on Southern Avenue and 16th Street. Dave and Ann Bennett are the church planters. For more information about the church, click on their website.

Temple, Texas

The Temple Project is now a mission church led by Pastor David Rapp. Redeemer Presbyterian Church has been meeting for over six months and they have already outgrown their initial meeting space.

Weatherford, Texas

Lou Tiscione is in Weatherford, Texas, and pastors a growing PCA church there. They are currently meeting on Sunday mornings and hope to become a particularized church in 2011. They are currently hosting a number of men’s studies with a focus on Christian leadership. They recently received an anonymous commitment to fund the remaining stained glass in their church building. They are also nearing completion of their Education Building, as well as their Sanctuary and Fellowship Hall, all of which are historic buildings in downtown Weatherford.

If you would like to learn more about Weatherford Presbyterian Church, you are welcome to email Lou.
